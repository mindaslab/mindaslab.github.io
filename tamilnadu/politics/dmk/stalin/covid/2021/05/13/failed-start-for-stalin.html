Failed start for Stalin

May 13, 2021

Tamilnadu   Politics   DMK   Stalin   Covid

I would rather say that M.K.Stalin is having a failed start of his CM post. It was funny when he announced lockdown, he himself ignored Corona and campaigned shamelessly, more of his shamelessness was revealed when he asked for donations from people for the CM fund to fight Corona.

He would gather people and ask votes and spread the disease, then when he comes to power he will say the disease has gone beyond control, would not take responsibility for it, then ask for money to correct his mistake. It’s a miserable failure of leadership.

Now coming to the lockdown. His implementation of the lockdown is funny. It’s night 11:12 now, and the ban was supposed to be in place since 12 noon, just now a bike passed my door. Where the heck is the ban? Just an hour ago I think the local DMK leader car (without flag) crossed my house in Sithalapakkam. News reports of heavy traffic in Saidapet and Guindy Chennai. He put an excellent policy, and just like Modi he is unable to implement it.

They say 10,000 police are in Chennai implementing the ban, lol! I wonder if the account books are recording crores of rupees are spent in implementing the ban.

I would rather say DMK itself is a failed party. It’s values are as follows:

  • Not to promote religion. But many in the party are religious.
  • To be democratic. You know how Stalin rose up to power.
  • To give equal chance at leadership to all. you know most leaders are from a few families.
  • To promote Tamil. This blog is written by a Tamilian and is in English. Kanimozhi’s school teaches in English.
  • Not to loot. We all know that DMK is THE KING of looting.

DMK is not a dravidian party, its a party to make Udhayanidhi Stalin the Chief minister because no one in DMK has worked as hard for the party :D. All other party member’s except Udhyanidhi have no balls it seems.

We need a party that is Democratic, Drvidian, Atheistic, that grows Tamil and better’s the lifes of Tamilians, gives chance to people who love people, and bubbles up leaders than dynasty. Will Tamils ever wake up and create this change?
