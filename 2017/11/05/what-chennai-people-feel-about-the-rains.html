What Chennai people feel about the rains?

Nov 5, 2017

Chennai had good rains I would say, and as usual the government even though it was warned by the met department and the opposition to be prepared for rains, let the city flood. We the Chennai citizens are unhappy about it.

Do you think Tamilnadu government was prepared for #ChennaiRains2017 ?

— Karthikeyan A K (@karthik_ak) November 3, 2017

I just conducted a poll on twitter and it wasn’t astonishing that most people feel that the government wasn’t prepared for it. Whats astonishing for me was 22% felt that government was prepared for it! Thats a huge number infact. If these are the people who are going to vote for AIADMK like fanatics, who knows it would come to power once again.

Another poll was about IT companies in Chennai. IT companies are worst managed things. They are extremely inefficient piece of management, thats because we get cheap labor in Chennai and hence they can be abused a lot. In fact slaves in ancient Rome were able to work for just 6 hours and were able to get away and they had 4 months of holiday! But we are forced to work so much.

This was the poll I conducted:

Did your IT company in #chennai made Saturday working because of #ChennaiRains2017 ?

— Karthikeyan A K (@karthik_ak) November 3, 2017

and not surprisingly looks like 50% of IT companies are inefficient and force their employees to work on Saturday because of the rain.
