The North has always fancied conquering the South for long. If you take Ramayanam, its about a victory of an unworthy destitute from northern India over a fine ruler who ruled the South. In fact people of Chennai and wealthy community of India, who were fed up by Mughals aided the British with wealth in conquering us. But at no time we lost our language.

During the Telugu (Kalappar) conquest, we lost our old Tamil script and moved to new one. This was refined during Periyaar times as we needed something to take advantage of the modern printing press, no longer we need to write on palm leaves, so we can leave out squiggly like characters that appear in languages like Malayam to Burmes which were formulated to be written on tender leaves. But all the time Tamil was Tamil. It’s even said that if Thiruvaluvar popped into existence today, he would understand what we speak.

Tamil had a grammar and reached perfection state (செம்மொழி) even like 5000 years ago. We eliminated imperfect pronunciations like sha, ha, gha, ish, long ago, possibly even before Prakrit came into being, possibly we could have done it in Mesopotamia (modern Iraq) where we got our first written form. Yes cuneiform is a Dravidian script, it was called Tal (தள்), which means push in Tamil and other Dravidian language, because we pushed sticks into clay tablets to create it. Possibly Dravidians who came to India discovered their own writing very late after that. Today Hindi still has these incomplete pronunciations. Imagine the cognitive gap.

After we came below Vindhyas, Dravidians became very good at resisting things from north, possibly our war techniques improved. That’s why we still exists as Tamils till today. Rajaraja’s invasion of these places (with ease) for spreading descent and Terrorism in Tamilnadu shocked Kerala like hell and created centuries of hate. Imagine, schools created by disciple of God been sacked like blowing dust away. Such super might, earned respect for Tamils.
