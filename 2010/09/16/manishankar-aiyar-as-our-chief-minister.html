Manishankar Aiyar as our Chief Minister

Manishankar Aiyar

I have had enough with this DMK and AIADMK rule in Tamilnadu. Many people of my age haven’t yet seen a non Dravidian rule Tamilnadu. I am desperate for a change. There is no way that a third alliance is going to sweep into power in our state. Though Vijayakanth has started to make half voices, the money power amassed by the DMK has already assured them virtual victory in coming state election.

One thing is clear, Congress and DMK won’t split. DMK is too afraid of Jaya and Vijaykanth. And Karuna desperately wants to see Stalin as CM. If that’s not possible he at the least wants Stalin to be safe from the iron fist of Jaya. If that lady comes to power, then DMK family empire is in trouble.

I like EVKS Illangovan very much. He is bold, ready to challenge old ways, not afraid to hit the top. He recently demanded if DMK alliance comes to power, then why not congress get the Chief minister seat? It’s a good demand. Karuna has always said that he or DMK never decided to stay in power and their goal is to serve the people, then why not hand over the CM seat to the congress? It will be a good decision as Congress has relatively good people compared to those in DMK.

If you ask me who from congress should be the CM? I would say it must be Manishankar Aiyar. The following are the reasons why I like him.

I hope Tamilians will be smart, voice their opinions on blogs, tweets and facebook. I wish all those who feel Mani should be our next CM will support this blog.
