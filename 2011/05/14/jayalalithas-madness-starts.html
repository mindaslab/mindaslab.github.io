Jayalalitha’s madness starts

Jayalalitha

I tuned into Jaya T.V yesterday to watch the poll analysis. Rather than seeing people who blah blah about those numbers rolling on screen, I was shocked to see astrologers who were blahing about how the astrology, the planet and all those stuff favored Jaya becoming the C.M. WHAT? WHAT? WHAT WHAT WHAT? Hello, India is a democracy (we like to call like that), not an astro-cacy.

All know jaya is a mad woman who’s mood swings this way and that way. Even her party cadres hate her and think AIADMK will do better if there is a better leader. Its not uncomman to see an unmarried person above 30 to be with hysteria. So thats understandable. But I never thought she will be mad enough to put astrologers to talk about poll results. LOL!

And this secretariat thing. She wants to shift it back to the old army fort on the beach. So this is the sign. Just because she hates Karunanidhi, she scraps all the good or bad things he had done for us. The new secretariat is a status symbol. It represents rising Tamilnadu. We are one of the best states in the Indian union. From an Hindi dominated nation we have transformed the political landscape so that the center cannot survive without Tamil power. What the heck a Kannadian will know about Tamilian feelings? Heck with Jaya.

I don’t know what all will happen in this five year term. I am anticipating  even stinging bloggers against Jaya too will be put behind bars in this rule. Remember how N.Ram of THE HINDU was hunted down by Jaya in her last tenure?

No matter what, five years from now I think we will vote out Jaya as we have voted out Karuna now. The only question is how much suffering we will endure in this tenure?
