If you have something that you feel the world must know, nothing beat the torrents. I have written  a programming book named 'I Love ruby'. Its 2015 edition which is on the making has received 85 hits on dropbox, who's statistics can be seen here http://goo.gl/#analytics/goo.gl/1qrMUN/all_time . On the other hand I launched it on the pirate bay which can be accessed from here http://thepiratebay.se/torrent/10198799/I_Love_Ruby_-_Free_Programming_Book

I started my transmission using the torrent program, at first there were about 129 leachers ( people who want to download) for my book which can be seen here

When I started seeding, quiet literally for few minutes and then came back this was the figure

There were only 11 people needing to download, and 84 people already had it! If you had seen the Google analytics URL, of all these days only 85 users have downloaded the book in 13 days. Whereas in The Pirate Bay that number was reached in seconds or at the max in 5 minutes. And how much data I had transmitted? Don't be baffled, see the image below

I had to transmit 2.99 MB to deliver a book of 2.12 MB to 80+ people!!!! Torrent technology is by no means less than a miracle. Its a boon to humanity and its shame on cinema and music companies to shun and ban such a great technology by lobbying to politicians.

If you have a work and if you feel its worth for humanity to have it, publish it with The Pirate Bay. Its the best publishing house I have ever seen.
