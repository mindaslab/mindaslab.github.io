Just another dev day

Jan 30, 2012

Well this thing strikes every developer, he needs to code some thing, his brain thinks about the logic in a flash and he sits down to code. But then the web browser launches, facebook, twitter and diaspora are visited, status are updated. Its just a simple code and it could be done after lunch says the heart.

Then you hit on chat with a old friend and before you know, its time for lunch. You have a hearty meal, full of junk,oil, grease and spoilers. You are so full and fat and now you need to take rest to digest it. You wow to yourself you will hit code and debug it in a flash when this food thing is over.

You launch your computer, you will find you have downloaded via torrent a movie, book or some thing, and you begin to watch or read. When it ends, you need to tell about it to your friends and hit on social network once again. Some guy pops in chats saying that he agrees with you on the movie or book and you chat once again. Well your tummy groans and its time for tea.

You finish your tea and snacks and take rest, by that time the day is almost over. You start thinking of dinner. After all this logic could be coded in a flash and hence it needs a good celebration, you launch music and time flies. You nod your head to it and tap your feet, it time to close coding for the day. After all its just a simple logic that needs a simple code and it could be done tomorrow.
