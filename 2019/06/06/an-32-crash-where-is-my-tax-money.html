AN-32 Crash, Where is my tax money?

Jun 6, 2019

Well, another military plane crash in India. Our planes are not maintained, some how Narendra Modi’s, Amit Shaha’s, Rahul Gandhi’s planes are in tiptop condition and never crash. Imagine how good our nation would be if law of nature crashes politicians vehicle when ever he or she tells a lie. There wouldn’t be enough vehicles left in the world.

AN-32 crash sends a message to Pakistan too. They must realise that Indian government kills more Indian solders than its terrorist does. So it must shift its strategy to become more effective. Perhaps it can do us more harm if it funds our political parties than funding Madrasas.

We have always heard this, that our army is capable of dealing with any kind of invasion. Well, good, that means we must in a great, well oiled condition, but are we? Wonder where all those money that we pay for the military goes? Some how we don’t even know the Rafel prices, even though its our money.

Have you ever noticed? When ever there is a bad news Modi is not at all in the picture and media never questions where he is.
