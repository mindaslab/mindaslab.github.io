Coward India

May 9, 2019

I felt very bad last week or the beginning of this week, Hamas lauched rockets into Gaza and Israel within no time retaliated and cease fire was reached between two sides. Israel is a tiny tiny nation with a tiny population, yet the are able to defeat the entire Arab world, strike their enemies when ever they are hit. Here we are second massive nation, 6th largest economy, if Pakistan terrorist strikes, we need to wait, plan and execute action fearing their army. Even if we kill less than a 100 of them we celebrate as a surgical strike. Are we really a nation that can defend itself?

This Masood Azad gets listed as terrorist and we celebrate, what the heck if he gets listed as terrorist or not? We haven’t killed him yet, even at this very instant he keeps plotting against India and rest of the world on how to bomb and kill some one.

We are a impractical nation, we stay terror should be abolished and it hasn’t been abolished yet. From the 60’s we were saying we will abolish poverty, from the age of Periyaar we say we will do away with the caste system, we say politics and religion should be kept apart, we say bribes are illegal. Yet not one of the things we say and dream have materialized.

This phenomenon points to one thing: Indians are cowards, they say and they cannot do. Indian men should roam around wearing sarees and women should roam around wearing nothing. That’s what we are worth for.
